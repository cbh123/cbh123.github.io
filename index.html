Research

For my honors thesis, I worked on creating a new model for understanding the Hermann Grid Illusion, pictured right. The new model increased accuracy from 75% to 80%. The model I'm working on is based on the concept of "surround cell" interactions, described in this paper by Linsley, Mely, and Serre (2018). I am currently working towards publishing this research.
